Partnership with the Austrian Embassy

Together with the Austrian Embassy, we were challenged to develop a platform that was aimed to become the center of the Ukrainian - Austrian cultural year 2019.

#Austria Ukraine2019

The cultural cooperation

Together we developed a platform that contributes to build a united and peaceful Europe, combines creative potential and deepens scientific and cultural cooperation between Ukraine and Austria.

Special feature for the people with visual impairments.

The design National colors of both countries are used in design elements. Fast and intuitive navigation, interactive and bright layouts. Customized for the people with visual impairments.

Official platform opening

The ceremony was hosted by the Austrian Embassy and different guests such as diplomats, cultural figures, friends of the Embassy and journalists were invited to the event.
